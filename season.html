Season Overview

One of the important aspects of the game this year is the scoring and so-called “swing points”. For example, stacking high caps are worth 2 points but descoring them only changes the score by 1. Contrary to a cap focused robot, a flag scoring bot has the advantages of speed and consistency while the ability to play defense. One test that we performed was to analyze the efficiency of cap scoring bots vs flag focused bots. In turn, it takes much longer for a cap bot to align and score compared to a flag focused robot not even giving regards to defense. This year the game will feature more flag scoring robot while a occasional efficient cap scoring robot will feature in more competitive environments.
