Our robot

The robot drive train consists of a basic one to one sprocket ratio connected to a high speed motor. One sprocket is directly connected to the motor axle and another is connected to the axle that is going through the 4” omni-wheel. It is a H-base that is rigid and compact that has optimal speed (doesn’t tilt over and not being too slow). The driving component of the robot is a skid steer system that is for one side of the robot driving in order to turn. Our flipper system is a simple flipper that has speed and accuracy while having enough torque to flip the caps. The only inefficiency of the system is that it relies on standoffs to flip it instead of a 2-bar.
