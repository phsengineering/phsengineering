Our robot

Our robot uses a four motor drive, which allows us to be quick and agile on the course as we currently lack a lift and flywheel system, though this is in development.

There is also a small flipper arm in the front of the robot that allows it to reliably flip caps, allowing us to be an effective partner and defensive robot.
