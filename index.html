About our robotics program

Parkland High School's Engineering Club features three teams - 4001E, comprised of sophomores, 4001A, comprised of juniors, and 4001F, comprised of seniors. Our teams compete throughout the eastern Pennsylvania area. Students joining the engineering club already have experience with the engineering design process through Parkland's engineering courses through Project Lead the Way, including Introduction to Engineering Design, Principles of Engineering, Digital Electronics, Computer Integrated Manufacturing, and more.
