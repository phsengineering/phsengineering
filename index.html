Parkland High School Engineering Club

Parkland High School's Engineering Club features three teams - 4001E, comprised of sophomores, 4001A, comprised of juniors, and 4001F, comprised of seniors. Our teams compete throughout the eastern Pennsylvania area. Students joining the engineering club already have experience with the engineering design process through Parkland's engineering courses, which follow the Project Lead the Way curriculum. Our members have taken various STEM classes, such as Introduction to Engineering Design, Principles of Engineering, Digital Electronics, Computer Integrated Manufacturing, and others.

New members can join by completing at least one engineering course and then joining our informational meeting at the end of September, or contacting by our club advisor, Mr. Yocum. Our club is divided by grade level and compete as individual teams.
