Our robot design

Our robot consists of a fast four motor drive system, a lift system that can reliably place caps on high posts, and a flywheel capable of hitting the high flags, allowing us to play many different roles in the course of a match, from defense to active scoring. A variety of sensors allows us to also do well in autonomous, as we reliably hit all three flags on our side of the field.
